Crisostomo Ibarra Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin is a student from San Diego, after he returned to his home town, he wants to achieve his father's wish. A school, but he faces numerous obstacles and challengers from certain people.
Maria Clara Maria Clara de los Santos, the daughter of Padre Damaso. She is a woman of a high social status.
Kapitan Tiago Kapitan Tiago is a wealthy and influential man,which makes him a well respected person. He is also the one who adopted Maria Clara.
Padre Salvi Padre Salvi is a Friar in San Diego, he abuses his power of being a Friar/Priest then later on manipulates the townspeople.
Sisa Sisa is the mother of Basilio and Crispin, abused by her husband lost her sanity after her sons disappeared.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is the biological father of Maria Clara, and was the previous town curate before Padre Salvi got his role. He abuses his power being a friar/priest and has a tyrannical behavior towards Filipinos.
Pilosopo Tasio Pilosopo Tasio is an old scholar and people think he is an out of mind/ a mad man because of his studies.
Elias Elias is a mysterious man who lives on a boat with his wife, hiding from authorities.
Salome Salome is a young girl who lives in San Diego with her sweetheart Elias, she wants to move to Mindoro with Elias to meet her family have a normal life there.
